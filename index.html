The Third
Baltimore Hackathon

The 2013 Baltimore Hackathon still in planning stages. If you're interested in staying up to date about hackathons in the Baltimore area please join our Hack Baltimore Google Group. We're also always on the look for volunteers, organizers, and sponsors so if you're interested join Hack Baltimore or send an email and let us know!

What?

Build a hardware or software project from idea to prototype in a weekend!

You can participate individually or as a team.

When?

Sometime around Baltimore Innovation Week, so late September or early October.

Where?

TBD


Why?

Bring the community together and have a good time. And, we'll throw in some prizes as the icing on the cake, too.

Rules

The rules are simple. You have a weekend to build a hardware or software project.

How do I participate?

Help us organize by joinging the Hack Baltimore Google Group or wait till you can register!
